Last modified: July 4, 2025

Welcome to BeastCV ("BeastCV", "we", "us", or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This U.S.–only Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the mobile application BeastCV Resume Enhancer (the "App").

U.S.–only availability: The App is offered solely to individuals located in the United States. We do not market to, or knowingly collect data from, anyone outside the U.S.

Questions? Email us at eastcv2025@gmail.com or use the in-App support form.

I. SCOPE & DEFINITIONS

This Policy applies to U.S. residents who download or use the App. “Personal data” (or “personal information”) means information that identifies, relates to, describes, or could reasonably be linked to a particular consumer or household.

II. CATEGORIES OF INFORMATION WE PROCESS

  • (a) Functional Data
  • (b) Payment Information
  • (c) Automatically Processed Data
  • (d) Contact Information

(a) Functional Data

  • Resume Content. Résumé documents you scan or import (images, PDFs, TXT) and text extracted via on-device OCR.
  • AI Output. Enhanced résumé text and recommendations generated by OpenAI.
  • User Identifier (UUID) We generate and store a unique, randomly assigned identifier (UUID) when you install and activate the App. This UUID is not linked to your device, name, email, Apple ID, or any other personal data. It is used solely to determine whether a user has access to the paid (enhanced) version of the App. This UUID remains anonymous and cannot be used to identify or track you across apps or services.

Processing flow. OCR and caching occur entirely on your iPhone/iPad. Extracted text is transmitted via TLS to our U.S.-hosted AWS backend only for real-time processing; it is never stored at rest on our servers. As soon as the OpenAI response streams back to you, both the request and response are wiped from server memory.

(b) Payment Information

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your card details.

(c) Automatically Processed Data

We collect minimal diagnostics (user identifier) to maintain security and performance. Providers:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — hosting & runtime logs

(d) Contact Information

If you contact support, we collect the info you provide (name, email, screenshots).

Children’s Data Collection

Our App is intended for users above the age defined as a “child” under applicable privacy laws. Guardians are responsible for ensuring minors do not submit personal data through the App without legally valid consent.

We interpret the term “child” as individuals under 16 years old, unless local regulations establish a different age threshold or explicit parental authorization is provided. We do not knowingly collect information from users who qualify as “child”.

Should any personal data from a child be identified without proper consent, we will immediately remove it from our systems. If you suspect we may have collected information from or about a child through the App without guardian approval, please contact us immediately.

IV. DATA RETENTION

Résumé text is never stored on our servers. On-device cache is auto-cleared when the App is closed or terminated.

This UUID is not tied to your identity or device and is used solely to verify eligibility for premium features. It is not shared with third parties and is deleted upon uninstallation or subscription cancellation.
